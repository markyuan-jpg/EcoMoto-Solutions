## Black Hat Warnings (Never Do)

❌ Buy backlinks from Fiverr/SEO panels
❌ Submit to link farms
❌ Use Private Blog Networks (PBNs)
❌ Automated comment spam
❌ Duplicate content across sites
❌ Low-quality directory submissions (100+ per day)

---

## Quality Guidelines

| Good Backlink | Bad Backlink |
|--------------|--------------|
| Relevant industry | Irrelevant niche |
| Domain authority 30+ | DA < 10 |
| Editorial placement | Footer/sidebar |
| Unique content | Duplicate content |
| Natural anchor text | Exact match anchor |

## Monitor Toxic Links

1. Go to Google Search Console → Links
2. Review "Top linking sites"
3. If suspicious domains found:
   - Use Google Disavow Tool
   - Or contact site owner to remove
