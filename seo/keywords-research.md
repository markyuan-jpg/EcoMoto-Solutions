## Long-tail Keywords

### Pattern 1: Product + Use Case

```
electric tricycle for warehouse operations
electric motorcycle for passenger transport
electric cargo tricycle for food delivery
electric shuttle for tourist sites
battery pack for electric tricycle
```

### Pattern 2: Product + Purchase Intent

```
wholesale electric cargo tricycle manufacturer
OEM electric motorcycle supplier China
bulk electric tricycle purchase
electric vehicle supplier near me
custom electric tricycle factory
```

### Pattern 3: Product + Question/Problem

```
how to choose electric cargo tricycle
electric motorcycle cost vs petrol
electric tricycle battery life
how much is electric tricycle
electric vehicle charging time
```

### Pattern 4: Comparison

```
electric tricycle vs gasoline van
electric vs petrol motorcycle
hub motor vs mid motor electric
LFP vs lithium ion battery
```

## Search Intent Classification

| Intent | Description | Content Type |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Info** (To Learn) | Research, understand | Blog posts, guides |
| **Nav** (To Find) | Look for specific brand | Homepage, about |
| **Trans** (To Buy) | Ready to purchase | Product pages, CTAs |
| **Comm** (To Compare) | Evaluate options | Comparisons, specs |
